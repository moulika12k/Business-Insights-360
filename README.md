# 📊 Business Insights 360 – Atliq Hardware

## 🔍 Overview

Business Insights 360 is a data analytics project focused on delivering actionable insights for **Atliq Hardware**, a B2B hardware manufacturer. The objective was to transform raw data into a comprehensive dashboard that enables strategic decision-making and business growth analysis.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze historical sales and financial performance
- Track market share and profitability
- Present Year-over-Year (YoY) metrics
- Provide executive-level KPIs in an interactive dashboard

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **Power BI** – for data modeling and visualization
- **Power Query** – for ETL (Extract, Transform, Load)
- **DAX** – for calculated columns, measures, and KPIs
- **Excel** – for data exploration and pre-processing

---

## 📈 Key Features

- **Executive Summary Dashboard**: Net Sales, Gross Margin %, Net Profit %, YoY trends
- **Market Share Analysis**: Country-wise and overall market growth
- **Profitability Insights**: COGS, revenue vs. margin metrics
- **YoY Comparisons**: Net Sales YoY %, Revenue Growth %, Market Expansion

---

## 📊 KPIs Tracked

- Gross Margin %
- Net Sales YoY %
- Net Profit %
- COGS (Cost of Goods Sold)
- Total Revenue
- Market Share %

---

## 🧠 Skills Demonstrated

- Data cleaning and transformation
- Building relationships using star schema
- DAX formulas like `CALCULATE`, `SUMX`, `SWITCH(TRUE(),...)`
- Dashboard design with slicers, cards, matrices, and ribbon charts
- Business storytelling for non-technical users
